Filing other documents

During the beta service development you should continue to use WebFiling to file your company information online.

This includes

  • confirmation statements
  • company accounts
  • directors and company secretaries – for example, new appointments, resignations and changes to personal details
  • changing a company name
  • changing an accounting reference date

If you haven't used the WebFiling service before

You'll need to register a password and email address, then request an authentication code for your company - which is sent to your company's registered office by post, so allow plenty of time for delivery.
